Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of data associated with rogermorenoapp, Roger Moreno Consulting, and related Meta integrations.

How to Request Deletion

If you want data associated with your interaction with our Meta-connected business tools to be deleted, send a request to hello@rogermoreno.com with the subject line Data Deletion Request.

Please include enough information for us to identify the request, such as:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your Facebook Page, WhatsApp number, or other relevant Meta account reference
  • A short description of the data or account connection you want deleted

Alternative Removal Through Facebook

You may also remove connected apps and business integrations from your Facebook settings where applicable. Removing app access from Facebook may stop further data sharing with the app.

What Happens After a Request

Once we receive a valid deletion request, we will review it and delete applicable data that is not required for security, fraud prevention, recordkeeping, or legal compliance. If some information must be retained for those reasons, it will no longer be actively used except as required.

Response Time

We aim to respond to deletion requests within a reasonable time.

Contact

For any privacy or deletion-related question, contact hello@rogermoreno.com.
