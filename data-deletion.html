Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of data associated with rogermorenoapp, Roger Moreno Consulting, and related Meta integrations.

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of applicable data, send an email to hello@rogermoreno.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.

Information to Include

Please include enough information for us to identify your request, such as:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your Facebook Page, WhatsApp number, or other relevant Meta-related reference
  • A brief description of the data or connection you want deleted

What Happens Next

After receiving a valid request, we will review it and delete applicable data that is not required for security, fraud prevention, recordkeeping, or legal compliance.

Alternative Removal

Where applicable, you may also remove app access through your Facebook settings.

Contact

For privacy or deletion-related questions, contact hello@rogermoreno.com.
